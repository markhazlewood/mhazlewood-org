User experience design is a research-based design discipline which seeks to create delightful and effective experiences for the users of technology-based products. This includes anything from online shopping carts to the dashboard in your car.

Much like other disciplines, there are dozens of specific processes to choose from and there is no one size to fit all designers or teams. In general, however, there are a few common phases in any design lifecycle.
